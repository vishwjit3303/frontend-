Our Plants

Roses

Description: Known for their beauty and fragrance, roses are a classic choice for any garden. Available in various colors and varieties, they can brighten up any space. Sunlight: Full Sun Watering: Moderate Care Level: Medium

Succulents

Description: Low-maintenance and stylish, succulents are perfect for indoor and outdoor arrangements. They require minimal watering and come in various shapes and sizes. Sunlight: Indirect Light Watering: Low Care Level: Easy

Bonsai Trees

Description: Bonsai trees are miniature trees cultivated in small containers. They require precise care but are a rewarding hobby for those who appreciate detailed gardening. Sunlight: Partial Shade Watering: Regular Care Level: High

Herbs

Description: Grow your own fresh herbs at home with our collection. Whether you’re looking for basil, rosemary, or thyme, these plants are perfect for cooking enthusiasts. Sunlight: Full Sun Watering: Moderate Care Level: Easy

Ferns

Description: Ferns add lush greenery to any space and thrive in shaded areas. Their delicate fronds create a serene atmosphere in both indoor and outdoor settings. Sunlight: Shade Watering: High Care Level: Medium

Orchids

Description: Orchids are exotic flowers known for their vibrant colors and unique shapes. They add elegance to any space but require specific care to thrive. Sunlight: Indirect Light Watering: Low to Moderate Care Level: High

Cacti

Description: Cacti are hardy plants that store water in their thick, fleshy stems. They’re ideal for dry climates and need very little maintenance, making them perfect for busy individuals. Sunlight: Full Sun Watering: Low Care Level: Easy

Lavender

Description: Lavender is a fragrant herb known for its calming scent and purple flowers. It’s great for gardens and can be used in cooking or as a natural air freshener. Sunlight: Full Sun Watering: Moderate Care Level: Medium
